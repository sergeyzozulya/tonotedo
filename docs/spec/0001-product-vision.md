# Product vision

## Summary

ToNoteDo is a personal productivity app for one user: notes, tasks, and a calendar in a single, calm, keyboard-driven workspace. It is local-first and offline-first — your data lives on your machine as plain markdown, owned by you, with no forced cloud and no subscription. The shape is simple but composable: multi-level groups, entries with custom properties, and global or scoped tags, all extensible through plugins (providers and processors). It aims to feel like a fast, modern, beautiful editor — not a database, not a team tool, not an AI assistant pretending to know your life.

## Pillars

- local-first
- offline-first
- cross-platform (desktop and mobile)
- privacy-oriented
- keyboard-friendly
- clean and simple UI
- highly customizable
- markdown-based
- multi-level groups
- entries (custom properties)
- calendar
- tags (global / scoped)
- mentions (people)
- plugins (providers and processors)

## Anti-pillars

- no forced cloud / subscription
- no teams / enterprise
- no AI-everywhere
- no ugly database feeling
